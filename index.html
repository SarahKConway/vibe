Snyk + Snowflake Cortex

Secure Your AI with Snyk and Snowflake Cortex

Integrate Snyk's AI Trust Platform directly with Snowflake Cortex to build, deploy, and manage secure AI applications at scale, all within your Snowflake environment.

Why Integrate?

For Developers

Secure your AI models and applications from the start. Find and fix vulnerabilities in your notebooks, custom code, model dependencies, and the container images you use to run them.

For AppSec Engineers

Gain unprecedented visibility into the security of your AI/ML assets. Automate security scanning within your Cortex pipelines and secure the entire supply chain of your models.

For CISOs

Confidently govern and manage the risks of AI adoption. Get a unified view of security across your applications and AI models to protect your most critical data and IP within Snowflake.

Integration Setup

  1. In Snyk, connect to your source code repository (e.g., GitHub) where your Cortex project code and notebooks reside.
  2. Import the repositories containing your Snowflake Cortex applications, functions, and models.
  3. Configure Snyk to scan your Python code, dependencies (requirements.txt), and container base images for vulnerabilities.
  4. View security results and actionable fixes directly in Snyk to secure your AI applications before deployment.
